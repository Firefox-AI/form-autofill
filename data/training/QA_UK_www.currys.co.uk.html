    Currys flexpay explained

    Pay at your pace

    Currys flexpay is the easy way to spread the cost of life's tech.

    Pay interest free
    Pay in monthly instalments, interest free, over terms of 12, 24 or 36 months. By paying the agreed amount every month, you won’t have to pay any interest and your balance will be paid off by the end of the promotional period.

    Pay monthly, low interest
    Spread the cost of your tech over 48 months, with a lower purchase interest rate of 9.9%. Payments are split into equal monthly instalments spanning the agreed term.

    Pay monthly
    Pay in monthly instalments for purchases £99 and over. You can choose a payment term from 12 months to 36 months. Interest is charged at your personalised interest rate.

    Buy now, pay later
    Pay as much or as little as you like over the offer period (usually 6, 9 or 12 months). If you pay the full balance of your purchase within the set period, no interest will be charged. Any balance left to pay after the set period will accrue interest from the date of purchase.
