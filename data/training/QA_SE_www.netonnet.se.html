NetOnNet card: pay or apply and use directly

Close
The NetOnNet card is a flexible option for those who want to have full control over your purchases at NetOnNet. It has no annual fee and if you choose to pay the full amount when you receive your avi, it is completely free to use. You also get points on all your purchases and free express shipping when you pay with the card.

Benefits of the NetOnNet card
  • No annual fee
  • Always 30-60 days free payment time
  • Up to 12 months interest-free instalment
  • Points on all purchases
  • We offer the cheapest express option

If you don't have a card
If you do not already have the card, you will come to the application when you choose the NetOnNet card as a payment method. There you fill in your details and agree to the terms and conditions. The application is made momentarily and the purchase is made on your new card.

How to pay: all at once or a little at a time
The month after you make your purchase, a bank statement and an avi will be sent to you. When you pay your avi, you can either pay the full amount or choose a repayment period that suits you.

For a representative example of a credit purchase of SEK 1 000 and 12 months interest-free, the interest rate is 0%, monthly administration fee SEK 49 and a setup fee of SEK 295 will be added.
The effective interest rate will be 16.02% and a total to pay SEK 10 883 (buy day 15 per month). Note that the minimum monthly charge is 150 SEK.
