Form fields with * are required.
APO/FPO

1. Select "United States" in the Country field.

2. Enter APO or FPO in the "City" field.

3. Select your region from the "State" pull-down menu: Armed Forces (Africa, Canada, Europe, Middle East) Armed Forces America (excl. Canada), Armed Forces Pacific.

4. Enter your Zip Code.

Orders are shipped to APO addresses via USPS (through the military mail system). Shipments to APOs can take up to 45 additional days to arrive. Unfortunately, once the package enters the military mail system, tracking ceases to update for military security reasons. This means that we no longer have visibility of your order and cannot provide tracking information. Please also note that, because your order can only be delivered by USPS, you should not select any of the Express or Expedited delivery options at checkout. Packages with Express or Expedited delivery options WILL NOT BE DELIVERED TO APO, FPO, or DPO military addresses.
