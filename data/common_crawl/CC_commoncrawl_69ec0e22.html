TRVL lx includes many of the TRVL features caregivers have come to love with some additional features including an extended canopy, customizable recline, extended usage and increased wheel dimensions.

A lightweight travel system where innovative safety and sleek luxury features combine into a compact package that moves you and baby seamlessly through the city.

TRVL lx Features:

  • In it for the long haul providing smooth strolls from birth to 50 pounds
  • Near flat recline and calf support that raises to covert seat to a carriage position for keeping baby extra cozy
  • Creates a sleek looking travel system connecting directly with Nuna Pipa series car seats with just a click - no adapters needed.
  • Shadow reel recline provides one-handed, customizable recline adjustments with more upright options.
  • Folds easily with one hand and stands on its own when folded.
  • One handed steering and exceptional maneuverability make it a dream to push and turn.
Pipa Urbn Features:

  • 2-second* steel-reinforced pipaFIX™ rigid latch installation makes set up swift, simple and above all - safe.
  • Ultralight for on-the-go at just 7 lbs.
  • Auto-reclining foot that aids in proper installations in vehicles.
  • One-handed vehicle and stroller release for quick and easy in-and-outs.
  • Travel with ease with pipaFIX™ or vehicle seat belt- no base needed.
  • Integrated magnetic buckle holders help keep straps out of the way when buckling baby in.
  • Sky drape pulls down smoothly and attaches quietly with magnetics.



*Refer to instructions for any final adjustments

**Weight excludes canopy and infant insert
