Tuition Fees

Canadian Students:$7,279 - $18,054

Notes for Canadian Students:

Going to university will be one of the biggest and most important investments you will ever make. Understanding the costs involved and preparing a budget is a must. When thinking about university, remember to consider not just the tuition, but also books, supplies, living expenses, and personal spending money.

Expected costs: Tuition fees, books, and supplies: $7,241 to $9,009 (for Domestic Students) and $28,760- $37,505 (for International Students), depending on your program. Living expenses: Approximately $11,000, depending on on-campus or off-campus living.

Notes for International Students:

Going to university will be one of the biggest and most important investments you will ever make. Understanding the costs involved and preparing a budget is a must. When thinking about university, remember to consider not just the tuition, but also books, supplies, living expenses, and personal spending money.

Expected costs: Tuition fees: $28,500- $38,000, depending on your program and an additional $1,000 - $2,000 on books and supplies. Living expenses: Approximately $11,000, depending on on-campus or off-campus living.
